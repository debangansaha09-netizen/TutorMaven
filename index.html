What is TutorMaven?

A professional ecosystem that replaces messy WhatsApp threads and manual registers with automated attendance, fee tracking, and AI-powered learning tools.

The Vision

We empower independent educators with the same professional tools used by major institutions, ensuring every student has a transparent and safe learning journey.

Mission

To build India’s most trusted digital infrastructure for 1-on-1 and small-group education.

The Ecosystem

For Tutors

✓ Blue Tick: Official verification to build instant parent trust.
✓ AI Quiz Creator: Generate assessments from notes in seconds.
✓ Promotional Banner: Increase visibility across the platform.
✓ Attendance & Fees: One-tap marking with automated reminders.
✓ Direct Connect: Professional communication bridge with parents.

For Students

✓ Interactive Map: Find the best-rated tutors near you visually.
✓ Fee Transparency: Clear records of all past and upcoming payments.
✓ Verified Discovery: Search only for vetted, high-quality experts.
✓ Attendance Logs: Monitor your consistency and learning hours.
✓ Instant Interaction: Message and book sessions with a single tap.
